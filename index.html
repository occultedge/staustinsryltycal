St. Austin's Occultedge Payment Calculator

Important Rules and Information:

  • The minimum batch payment is calculated based on 12 hours per month at $13 per hour.
  • If the Occultedge share (20% of the total student fees) plus the 5% transfer charge is greater than the minimum batch payment, you will pay that higher amount.
  • The total student fees for each batch are calculated based on the selected payment type (e.g., registration or installments) and the number of students.
  • A 5% transfer charge is added to the Occultedge share.
  • The selected course does not affect the fee calculation but will be displayed for transparency purposes.
